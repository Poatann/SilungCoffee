ABOUT US

Silung is a cozy, pet-friendly café and bakery in Mabalacat City. We believe in creating a warm and welcoming space where you can enjoy freshly baked bread, delicious cakes, and specialty coffee — all while relaxing with your furry friends. We aim to be your peaceful retreat in the middle of the bustling city.
